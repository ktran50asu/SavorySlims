Chick-fil-A

12-Count Grilled Nuggets

~200 cal • ~38 g protein • ~2 g carbs • ~4.5 g fat

  • Order 12-count Grilled Nuggets.
  • Skip sauces or use a zero/very-low-calorie option.
  • Add a fruit cup if you need carbs.

Chipotle

Double Chicken Bowl (no rice/beans)

~400 cal • ~64 g protein • ~5–10 g carbs • ~10–12 g fat

  • Bowl + Double Chicken.
  • Add Fajita Veggies, Tomatillo Green-Chili Salsa, Romaine.
  • Skip rice/beans/cheese/sour cream/guac to keep calories low.

Panda Express

Grilled Teriyaki Chicken + Super Greens

~430 cal • ~45 g protein • ~14–22 g carbs • ~13 g fat

  • Bowl with Super Greens side.
  • Grilled Teriyaki Chicken; sauce on the side, use lightly.

KFC

Grilled Chicken Breast + Green Beans

~235 cal • ~39 g protein • ~5 g carbs • ~7 g fat

  • Kentucky Grilled Chicken Breast (not fried) + Green Beans.
  • Skip biscuits and creamy sides.

Subway

Rotisserie-Style Chicken Protein Bowl

~220–320 cal • ~31–38 g protein • ~7–12 g carbs • ~8–16 g fat

  • Choose the Rotisserie-Style Chicken Protein Bowl.
  • Load veggies; skip cheese and dressing (or choose light vinaigrette).
