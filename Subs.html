Carne Asada Tacos (Lean)

Ingredients (3 tacos, 1 serving)

  • Lean top sirloin, trimmed, 200 g cooked (lime, garlic, salt, pepper)
  • Corn tortillas, 3 small
  • White onion, 1/4 cup, finely diced
  • Cilantro, 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Guacamole, 1 Tbsp
  • Fresh lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Sear steak: Very hot cast-iron or grill pan. Sear 3–4 min per side to ~135°F (57°C). Rest 5 min, slice thin across the grain.
  2. Warm tortillas: Dry-skillet or open flame 20–30 sec/side until pliable.
  3. Assemble: Divide steak across tortillas. Top with onion, cilantro, 1 Tbsp guac total, and lime juice.

Macros (approx.)

~560 kcal • 60 g protein • 45 g carbs • 18 g fat

Healthy Pepperoni Pizza (Personal)

Ingredients (1 personal pizza)

  • Very thin flatbread/pita crust, 1 (≈150–200 kcal)
  • Low-fat mozzarella, shredded, 75–85 g
  • Turkey pepperoni, 40 g (≈15 slices)
  • Tomato sauce (no added sugar), 1/4 cup
  • Dried oregano & garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Spread sauce, add cheese & pepperoni; season.
  3. Bake 8–10 min until cheese bubbles and edges are crisp.
  4. Cool 2 min; slice into 4 wedges.

Macros (approx.)

~710 kcal • 60 g protein • 50 g carbs • 26 g fat

In-N-Out Style Burger (Healthy)

Ingredients (1 burger)

  • 93% lean ground beef, raw, 170 g (form 3/4" patty)
  • Light whole-wheat bun, 1 (≈120–140 kcal)
  • Lettuce leaf, 1; tomato, 2 slices
  • Yellow peppers, grilled, 2 strips
  • Onion, sliced, 1/4 cup, caramelized (light spray)
  • Healthy Spread: nonfat Greek yogurt 2 Tbsp + light mayo 1 tsp + ketchup 1 tsp + relish 1 tsp
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Onions: Medium heat, tiny spray + pinch salt, 10–12 min to golden.
  2. Patty: Med-high, 3–4 min/side to 160°F internal.
  3. Bun: Toast 1–2 min cut-side down.
  4. Assemble: Spread half the sauce → lettuce → tomato → patty → onions & peppers → remaining sauce.

Macros (approx.)

~705 kcal • 58 g protein • 45 g carbs • 28 g fat
