Carne Asada Tacos (Lean)

Ingredients (3 tacos)

  • Lean top sirloin, trimmed, 200 g cooked
  • Corn tortillas, 3 small
  • White onion, 1/4 cup, cilantro 1/4 cup, lime wedges
  • Guacamole, 1 Tbsp

Instructions

  1. Sear steak 3–4 min/side to ~135°F; rest 5 min; slice thin.
  2. Warm tortillas 20–30 sec/side.
  3. Assemble; use 1 Tbsp guac total; finish with lime.

Macros: ~560 kcal • 60 g protein • 45 g carbs • 18 g fat

Healthy Pepperoni Pizza (Personal)

Ingredients

  • Very thin flatbread/pita, 1
  • Low-fat mozzarella, 75–85 g
  • Turkey pepperoni, 40 g
  • Tomato sauce, 1/4 cup, oregano & garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Bake at 425°F for 8–10 min until cheese bubbles and edges are crisp.

Macros: ~710 kcal • 60 g protein • 50 g carbs • 26 g fat

In-N-Out Style Burger (Healthy)

Ingredients

  • 93% lean ground beef, 170 g; light whole-wheat bun, 1
  • Lettuce, tomato, caramelized onions (spray), yellow peppers
  • Yogurt 2 Tbsp + light mayo 1 tsp + ketchup 1 tsp + relish 1 tsp

Instructions

  1. Caramelize onions 10–12 min over medium heat.
  2. Cook patty 3–4 min/side to 160°F; toast bun 1–2 min.
  3. Assemble with veggies and spread.

Macros: ~705 kcal • 58 g protein • 45 g carbs • 28 g fat
