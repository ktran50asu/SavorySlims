Chicken Teriyaki & Rice

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • Chicken breast, raw, 200 g (≈7 oz)
  • White rice, raw, 1/3 cup (yields ~1 cup cooked)
  • Broccoli florets, 1 cup
  • Olive oil, 1 tsp
  • Low-sodium teriyaki sauce, 1 Tbsp
  • Salt, black pepper

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Rice: Rinse 1/3 cup rice. Add 2/3 cup water, bring to boil, cover, low heat 15 min; rest 5 min.
  2. Chicken: Pat dry, season. Skillet with 1/2 tsp oil over med-high; 5–6 min per side to 165°F. Rest 3 min; slice.
  3. Broccoli: Microwave with 2 Tbsp water, covered, 2–3 min; drain.
  4. Assemble: Rice → chicken → broccoli; drizzle teriyaki + remaining 1/2 tsp oil.

Macros (approx.)

~705 kcal • 70 g protein • 60 g carbs • 24 g fat

Ground Beef & Potatoes Skillet

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 93% lean ground beef, raw, 300 g (yields ~240 g cooked)
  • Russet potato, 200 g, diced 1/2-inch
  • Olive oil, 1 tsp
  • Onion, 1/4 cup, diced
  • Red bell pepper, 1/4 cup, diced
  • Nonfat Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup
  • Salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Roast potatoes: 425°F; toss with 1/2 tsp oil, salt, paprika. Roast 20 min, flip, 10 min more.
  2. Beef: Skillet med-high 7–8 min to brown. Add onion/pepper, season; 3–4 min more. Drain if needed.
  3. Serve: Potatoes on plate, beef on top, yogurt dollop.

Macros (approx.)

~703 kcal • 62 g protein • 48 g carbs • 33 g fat

Seared Steak & Broccolini

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • Sirloin steak, raw, 280 g (yields ~240 g cooked)
  • Broccolini, 1 cup, trimmed
  • Jasmine rice, raw, 1/4 cup (yields ~2/3 cup cooked)
  • Olive oil, 1 tsp
  • Salt, black pepper, garlic powder

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Rice: Rinse 1/4 cup rice; 1/2 cup water; simmer 12 min; rest 5 min.
  2. Steak: Season. Skillet 1/2 tsp oil, med-high; 4 min first side, 3–4 min second (135°F). Rest 5 min; slice.
  3. Broccolini: Same pan, 1/2 tsp oil + 2 Tbsp water; cover 4–5 min until tender.
  4. Serve: Rice base, sliced steak, broccolini.

Macros (approx.)

~712 kcal • 61 g protein • 38 g carbs • 36 g fat

Salmon Poke Bowl

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • Sushi-grade salmon, raw, 200 g
  • Sushi rice, raw, 1/4 cup (yields ~2/3 cup cooked)
  • Edamame, 1/2 cup, shelled
  • Avocado, 1/4 fruit
  • Red cabbage, 1/2 cup, shredded
  • Cucumber, 1/2 cup, sliced
  • Soy sauce, 1 Tbsp; lemon wedge; sesame seeds

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Rice: Rinse 1/4 cup rice; 1/3 cup water; simmer 12 min; rest covered 10 min.
  2. Salmon: Cube 1/2"; toss with soy + sesame; chill.
  3. Edamame: Boil 3–4 min; drain.
  4. Veg: Slice avocado, cucumber; shred cabbage.
  5. Assemble: Rice base; arrange salmon, edamame, avocado, cucumber, cabbage; lemon squeeze.

Macros (approx.)

~700 kcal • 65 g protein • 45 g carbs • 27 g fat
