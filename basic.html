Chicken Teriyaki & Rice

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • Chicken breast, raw, 200 g (≈7 oz)
  • White rice, raw, 1/3 cup (yields 1 cup cooked)
  • Broccoli florets, 1 cup
  • Olive oil, 1 tsp
  • Low-sodium teriyaki sauce, 1 Tbsp
  • Salt, black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook rice: Rinse 1/3 cup rice. Add 2/3 cup water, bring to boil, cover, reduce heat, cook 15 minutes, rest 5 minutes.
  2. Cook chicken: Pat dry, season. Heat skillet with 1/2 tsp oil on medium-high. Cook 5–6 min per side until 165°F inside. Rest 3 min, slice.
  3. Steam broccoli: Microwave with 2 Tbsp water, covered, for 2–3 min until tender.
  4. Assemble: Rice base, chicken slices, broccoli on side. Drizzle teriyaki + remaining oil.

Macros

~705 kcal • 70 g protein • 60 g carbs • 24 g fat

Ground Beef & Potatoes Skillet

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 93% lean ground beef, raw, 300 g
  • Russet potato, 200 g, diced 1/2-inch
  • Olive oil, 1 tsp
  • Onion, 1/4 cup, diced
  • Red bell pepper, 1/4 cup, diced
  • Nonfat Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup
  • Salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Roast potatoes: Toss with oil, salt, paprika. Roast at 425°F for 20 min, flip, roast 10 min more.
  2. Cook beef: Heat skillet medium-high. Add beef, cook 7–8 min until browned. Add onion + pepper, season, cook 3–4 min more.
  3. Assemble: Plate potatoes, top with beef mix, dollop of Greek yogurt.

Macros

~703 kcal • 62 g protein • 48 g carbs • 33 g fat

Seared Steak & Broccolini

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • Sirloin steak, raw, 280 g
  • Broccolini, 1 cup
  • Jasmine rice, raw, 1/4 cup
  • Olive oil, 1 tsp
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Rice: Cook 1/4 cup rice with 1/2 cup water, simmer 12 min, rest 5 min.
  2. Steak: Season well. Heat skillet medium-high with 1/2 tsp oil. Sear 4 min one side, 3–4 min other side (135°F for medium rare). Rest 5 min, slice.
  3. Broccolini: In same pan, add 1/2 tsp oil + 2 Tbsp water. Cover, steam-sauté 4–5 min until tender.
  4. Assemble: Rice base, sliced steak, broccolini side.

Macros

~712 kcal • 61 g protein • 38 g carbs • 36 g fat

Salmon Poke Bowl

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • Sushi-grade salmon, raw, 200 g
  • Sushi rice, raw, 1/4 cup
  • Edamame, 1/2 cup
  • Avocado, 1/4 fruit
  • Red cabbage, 1/2 cup, shredded
  • Cucumber, 1/2 cup, sliced
  • Soy sauce, 1 Tbsp
  • Lemon wedge, sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Rice: Rinse 1/4 cup rice. Cook with 1/3 cup water, simmer 12 min, rest 10 min.
  2. Salmon: Cube salmon 1/2-inch. Toss with soy sauce + sesame seeds. Chill.
  3. Edamame: Boil 3–4 min, drain.
  4. Veg: Slice avocado, cucumber, shred cabbage.
  5. Assemble: Rice base. Arrange salmon, edamame, avocado, cucumber, and cabbage. Garnish lemon + sesame.

Macros

~700 kcal • 65 g protein • 45 g carbs • 27 g fat
