Chicken Teriyaki & Rice

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • Chicken breast, raw, 200 g (≈7 oz)
  • White rice, raw, 1/3 cup (≈1 cup cooked)
  • Broccoli florets, 1 cup
  • Olive oil, 1 tsp; low-sodium teriyaki, 1 Tbsp; salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Rice: 2/3 cup water, simmer 15 min; rest 5 min.
  2. Chicken: skillet + 1/2 tsp oil, 5–6 min/side to 165°F; rest 3 min; slice.
  3. Broccoli: microwave with 2 Tbsp water, covered, 2–3 min; drain.
  4. Assemble and drizzle teriyaki + remaining oil.

Macros: ~705 kcal • 70 g protein • 60 g carbs • 24 g fat

Ground Beef & Potatoes Skillet

Ingredients

  • 93% lean ground beef, raw, 300 g (≈240 g cooked)
  • Russet potato, 200 g, diced 1/2"
  • Olive oil, 1 tsp; onion & pepper, each 1/4 cup; seasonings
  • Nonfat Greek yogurt, 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. Roast potatoes 425°F: 20 min, flip, 10 min more.
  2. Brown beef 7–8 min; add veg 3–4 min; drain if needed.
  3. Plate potatoes → beef → yogurt.

Macros: ~703 kcal • 62 g protein • 48 g carbs • 33 g fat

Seared Steak & Broccolini

Ingredients

  • Sirloin steak, raw, 280 g (≈240 g cooked)
  • Broccolini, 1 cup
  • Jasmine rice, raw, 1/4 cup
  • Olive oil, 1 tsp; salt, pepper, garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Rice: 1/2 cup water; simmer 12 min; rest 5.
  2. Steak: 1/2 tsp oil; 4 min first side, 3–4 min second (135°F); rest 5; slice.
  3. Broccolini: same pan, 1/2 tsp oil + 2 Tbsp water; cover 4–5 min.

Macros: ~712 kcal • 61 g protein • 38 g carbs • 36 g fat

Salmon Poke Bowl

Ingredients

  • Sushi-grade salmon, 200 g; sushi rice, raw, 1/4 cup
  • Edamame 1/2 cup, avocado 1/4, red cabbage 1/2 cup, cucumber 1/2 cup
  • Soy sauce 1 Tbsp; lemon wedge; sesame

Instructions

  1. Cook rice (1/3 cup water) 12 min; rest covered 10.
  2. Cube salmon; toss with soy + sesame; chill.
  3. Boil edamame 3–4 min; prep veg; assemble; finish with lemon.

Macros: ~700 kcal • 65 g protein • 45 g carbs • 27 g fat
